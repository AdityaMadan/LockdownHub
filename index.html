Coronavirus disease or COVID-19 is an infectious disease. It was declared as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern by the World Health Organisation(WHO) on 30 January 2020.

While the outbreak started in China, it has spread to the world’s leading economies like the USA and UK. It has also affected growing economies like India and Italy and has resulted in a nation-wide lockdown in both these countries. Self-quarantine and staying at home are two systems that are being followed at a global level to fight this monstrosity.
